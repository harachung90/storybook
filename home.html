The Monk Diamond is one of the rarest and most valuable diamonds in Europe. It has a fascinating history. It was owned by Russian nobility, stolen by a famous criminal gang and then found hidden in remote mountains in one of the most sensational discoveries of the century. Find out more about the Monk Diamond in this website or come and see it for yourself at the House of Volkov in Moscow.
